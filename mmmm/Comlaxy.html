Appearance

He is a skeleton wearing white shirt and dark red colored jacket. He wears black trousers with dark red stripes. His eyes are a circle made by 1 ring which is color dark red. This depends on his form. On the normal form he only has 1 ring to form his eyes. On form 2 he was two rings with distance always 1 cm from the outer ring which varies because if his eye shrinks then the distance shrinks too. And so on but on form 8 he has a full circle with a diamond shape on the middle.

Personality

He is a comedy lord who doesn’t seem to care about almost any situation and also is very sarcastic. He does get serious if the situation is beyond worst.

Power

Power (physical power) depends on forms.

normal form

He is supersonic and also has superhuman strength capable of lifting entire buildings. One being named “Tsu” fought him while Comlaxy was not serious at all and he casually threw her around. Tsu was capable of creating craters on the moon.

form 2

His speed is 20% c and he is capable of creating kms wide craters with a punch. A punch from him can obliterate city level beings.

form 3

His speed is now FTL+ and he is capable of obliterating star clusters with a punch. A punch from him has killed multiple solar system level beings.

form 4

His speed is now infinite and he is capable of destroying (fake) galactic super clusters with a punch. A punch from him has killed multiple (fake) galactic super cluster level beings. Another being named “Gingakei No Kami” also known as “Cosmic God” fought Comlaxy and Comlaxy casually one punched Gingakei No Kami/Cosmic God.

form 5

His speed is immeasurable and he is capable of destroying portions of the (fake) universe with a punch. A punch from him has killed multiple (fake) low-universal beings.

form 6

His speed is irrelevant and he is capable of destroying the whole (fake) universe with a punch. A punch from him has killed many (fake) high-universal beings. He defeated “God” in this form.

form 7

His speed is beyond time itself and he is capable of destroying entire (fake) multiverses with a punch and he is powered by the core of the (fake) multiverse. He has killed multiple (fake) multiversal gods. He defeated “Maruchibāsu no Kami”.

form 8

His speed is capable of going infinite distance with zero time frame and he is capable of destroying entire (fake) hyperverses with a punch and also capable of shaking the (fake) Omniverse with a punch. He has fought “Akuma” also known as “The Omniversal God” and their fight destroyed the (fake) Omniverse but was later fixed by Miany himself.
