Appearance

Anotam appears as an ordinary human with blue eyes and with white hair and dark coat. His appearance on form 2 has glowing blue eyes. Form 2 and 3 appearance are the same. His appearance on form 4 his body has a dark scale-like large patterns which function like hair and stand up a little bit and his skin is hard and is dark and his eyes’ sclera is dark and his iris is also dark and his pupils are dark red.

Personality

His humour is dark and he jokes on purpose to mock his enemies. He is chaotic.

Power

normal form

He has superhuman strength and super speed. He can pick up large buildings.

form 2

His power is planetary level and his punches can destroy planets.

form 3

His power is now (fake) hyperversal and he has portal creation, matter manipulation, teleportation and energy beams that can destroy (fake) hyperverses.

form 4 (“Omniversal Anotam”)

This form was made when Transcencia gave him all the power in the (fake) Omniverse which made him the strongest being in the (fake) Omniverse itself. He was able to destroy and create the (fake) Omniverse without effort.
